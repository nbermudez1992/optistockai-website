**AGaaS Demand Planning for LATAM SMEs**  
*Chief of Staff: Jaime | Last Updated: 2026-03-19*

---

## 🎯 The Core Problem We Solve

LATAM SMEs (especially retailers) have:
- **30-90 day inventory cycles** with significant upfront capital
- ERPs like **Alegra & Siigo** that handle accounting/operations but have ZERO demand planning
- No way to optimize stock without expensive consultants
- Cash trapped in excess inventory

**Our solution:** Agentic AI layer on top of their existing ERP → demand forecasting + inventory optimization at ~$99-199/month.

## 📍 Market Opportunity

### Colombia
- ~500,000 SMEs (主要: retail, fashion, electronics)
- Alegra: 50,000+ active users
- Siigo: 40,000+ active users
- Target: **Bogotá, Medellín, Cali** retailers with 20-200 employees

### Mexico
- ~4M SMEs
- Target: **CDMX, Guadalajara, Monterrey** retail
- Existing list: 88+ clothing/fashion retailers in CDMX (from mexico_retail_companies.csv)

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- **Company:** Retailer, wholesaler, fashion/textile
- **ERP:** Uses Alegra, Siigo, or similar (SAP Business One, Megacable)
- **Pain:** 30-90 day inventory cycles, cash trapped, stockouts or overstock
- **Size:** 10-200 employees, $200K-$10M revenue
- **Geography:** Colombia (Bogotá, Medellín) + Mexico (CDMX, GDL, MTY)
