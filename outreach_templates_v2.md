# OptiStockAI — Outreach Templates (v2)
**Version 2 — Retailer-Focused, Multi-Store ICP | 2026-03-19**

---

## 🎯 ICP for Outreach
- **Company:** Retailer with 2-5 physical stores
- **Markets:** Colombia (Bogotá, Medellín) + Mexico (CDMX, Guadalajara)
- **Industry:** Apparel, fashion, accessories, specialty retail
- **Pain:** Inventory cycles 30-90 days, cash trapped in stock, no demand forecasting
- **Solution:** OptiStockAI — AI demand planning connected to their ERP (Alegra/Siigo)

```
¿Tienes 10 min esta semana para contarte cómo funciona?

Saludos,
Nicolas

```
